SUBSCRIPTION BILLING

All subscriptions are billed on a recurring monthly or annual basis. Charges are processed on the same calendar day each month as the original sign-up date. If the charge date falls on a weekend or holiday, the charge will be processed on the next business day. Users will receive an email notification 48 hours before each recurring charge. Subscription fees include applicable taxes which vary by jurisdiction. Upgrading a subscription mid-cycle results in a prorated charge for the remainder of the billing period. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Cancelling a subscription stops all future charges but does not trigger a refund for the current period unless within the 7-day refund window. Duplicate charges may occur if a payment method fails and is retried; any duplicate charges will be refunded automatically within 3 business days.
