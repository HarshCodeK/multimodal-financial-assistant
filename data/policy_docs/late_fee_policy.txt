LATE FEE POLICY

Late fees are applied to any payment not received by the due date shown on the invoice. A flat late fee of $15 or 1.5% of the outstanding balance, whichever is greater, is applied to accounts that are past due by more than 5 calendar days. An additional late fee of $10 is applied every 30 days thereafter until the balance is paid in full. Customers are notified via email when a late fee is assessed. Late fees may be waived one time per calendar year if the account was previously in good standing. Accounts that remain unpaid for more than 90 days may be referred to a third-party collections agency. Late payment history may affect credit reporting and eligibility for future promotional rates.
