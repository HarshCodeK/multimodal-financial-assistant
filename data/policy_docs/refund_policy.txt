REFUND POLICY

Refunds are issued within 30 days of the original purchase date. To be eligible for a refund, the item must be unused and in its original packaging. Digital products and services are non-refundable once accessed or downloaded. Subscription fees are prorated and refunded on a monthly basis if canceled within the first 7 days of the billing cycle. All refund requests must be submitted through the customer support portal. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and credited back to the original payment method. Partial refunds may be issued for damaged items at the company's discretion. Late payment fees and service charges are non-refundable. Customers with a history of excessive refund requests may be denied future refunds at the company's discretion.
